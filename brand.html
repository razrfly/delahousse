DELAHOUSSAYE ROSE

Appellation Côtes De Provence Protégée

  • General

    Exceptionally sensual, stylish and elegant, DelaHoussaye Rosé gives generously to all the senses. You first notice that the colour and clarity is breath taking, the pastel pamplemousse inviting, clear and sparking. On the nose it is a fragrant explosion of wild strawberry, delicate citrus, white flowers and lychee. It is a ‘full-mouth’ of fruits, cut with an extremely fresh and lively acidity. The minerality of the pedigree terrior is distinctive, complex, refreshing and present. A very exciting, unique and beautiful rosé of 2016.

  • Informations

    ORIGIN Côtes de Provence
    BLEND Grenache 60% and Syrah 40%
    SITE 300-400m altitude
    SOIL Clay-limestone
    VITICULTURE Certified Sustainable
    PERFORMANCE 50-55hl/hectare
    VINIFICATION Vin de Pressurage, short maceration, chilled settling and temperature controlled fermentation.
    ANALISIS Alcohol 12.6%/ pH 3.31/ SR 0.8 g / l
    BOTTLING 14 February 2017

  • Pairing

    Delahoussaye Rosé is exciting as an aperitif that continues beautifully with appetizers, charcuterie, seafood, bouillabaisse, Mediterranean plates, Asian, sushi, grills, white meats and salads.
